If it is a product you want UGC for, then you simply send your product to the business address we will provide. We will prepare a brief outlining the content we will produce for you, and after you approve the breif and we receive the product, we will aim to have the content filmed, edited and sent within 48 hours. We then send the content with a watermark (which is removed after approval). Once approved by you, we will remove the watermark and send the finished video(s). For service based videos, it is much the same without the need to send the product!

If for whatever reason you are not satisfied with the content, we offer a no question free revision based on your feedback. After the revision, if you are still not satisfied, we can continue making revisions or we can offer a full refund.

Not without permission. When you work with us, the content we produce for you is not shared with other clients, and once approved is owned entirely by you, to be used as you see fit.
